Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. KCV Electric is a fully licensed California electrical contractor (CSLB License #1131803) and we are fully insured and bonded. You can verify our license at cslb.ca.gov.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes — all estimates are free with no obligation. We come out, look at the work, and give you a clear written price before any job starts.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Modesto, CA and serve the entire Bay Area within a 100-mile radius. This includes Stockton, Tracy, Manteca, Turlock, Merced, Livermore, Pleasanton, Fremont, San Jose, Oakland, Hayward, Sacramento and surrounding cities.

Do you handle 24/7 emergencies?

Yes. We provide round-the-clock emergency electrical service — sparking outlets, power outages, exposed wires, burning smells. Call (209) 765-7013 any time, day or night.

Do you speak Spanish?

Yes — we provide bilingual service in English and Spanish. Just call and let us know which you prefer.

How much does an electrician cost?

Pricing depends on the job. Small repairs are usually quick and affordable; larger projects like panel upgrades or rewiring vary by scope. We give you a written estimate up front — no hidden fees, no surprises.

Can you install a Tesla or EV charger?

Yes. We install Tesla Wall Connectors, ChargePoint, and universal Level-2 EV chargers. We can also upgrade your electrical panel to handle the load if needed.

Do you pull permits?

Yes. We handle all required permits and inspections for work that requires them — panel upgrades, new circuits, service changes, and more. Everything is done to California electrical code.
